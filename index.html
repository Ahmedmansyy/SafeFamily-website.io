About App

App For Your Safety

Safe Family is an app that allows family members to check on each other's safety by tracking their location, such as when taking an Uber. With just one click, any family member can send an emergency message to the whole family. This app makes it easy for parents to keep track of their children. The app can also be used by elders to notify their children or grandchildren if they are not well, or something has happened. The requirements for the app were gathered through interviews with parents who expressed similar concerns about the safety of their children from kidnapping and their elders from sudden health issues.
